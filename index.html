About me

Game developer specialized in Unity and C#, focused on building dynamic and well-structured interactive experiences. I'm passionate about combining solid programming with strong visual design to create games that feel smooth, responsive, and engaging.

I have experience working with real-time VFX and Shader Graph in Unity, exploring how visual systems and gameplay logic can work together to enhance player immersion. I enjoy tackling technical challenges that require optimizing mechanics, improving performance, and refining every detail to deliver polished and functional experiences.
